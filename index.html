FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you handle sensitive data? Is my data going to be secure?

Based on the confidentiality of your data, we'd run it through an anonymization API and/or have participating data scientists sign a nondisclosure agreement.

What if I need improvements to a machine learning model?

We offer a subscription-based model, where we improve and train your models on new data at any time. Contact us for in-depth pricing details.

Why blockchain?

Being able to trust AI requires transparency and auditability in the sources of data that are used to train it, requiring open cooperation between the providers of training data, data scientists, and end users. These needs can be met by the immutable frameworks for trust provided by blockchain.

What can AI do for me?

AI, or more precisely machine learning, is a way to magnify your organizational capabilities by learning from your data, to do things like automating repetitive tasks, forecasting, discovering trends, and optimizing processes.

Can I recruit talent from the bitgrit Data Scientist Community?

bitgrit also acts as a recruiter, and can connect data scientists from our community to your organization and assess a fee upon hiring.

How does bitgrit pick the best solution from all submissions?

bitgrit facilitates machine learning solutions that are light-weight, easy-to-use, and minimize the technical know-how needed for maintenance, besides maximizing accuracy.

Where are you based?

We're an international team headquartered in Tokyo, Japan, with offices in New Delhi, India and Tallinn, Estonia.
