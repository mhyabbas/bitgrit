What We Do

What We Do

At present, bitgrit provides two central services:

an online community of data scientists and a competition platform where they can submit their AI solutions to companies with data and AI-related needs.

Online Community

Our online community of data scientists enables users to share information regarding datasets and AI training. This open, decentralized platform of communication creates a stronger intellectual community centered around collaboration.

To support our community, we host regular events around the world, including the World Data Science Forum.

Competition Platform

bitgrit's competition platform is where clients share problems requiring AI solutions - and in response, our networks of data scientists delivers valuable algorithms and insights.

In the future, we will roll out other functions to further our goal of democratizing AI: an AI marketplace, blockchain integration, job boards, and more.
